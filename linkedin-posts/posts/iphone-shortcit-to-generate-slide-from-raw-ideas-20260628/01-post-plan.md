## Core Insight
A single iPhone shortcut can eliminate the procrastination gap between rough notes and a structured slide deck by automating the first draft with your own custom AI prompt.

## Target Audience
Early-to-mid career software engineers and technical builders who capture ideas in notes or voice memos but consistently fail to act on them because the conversion to polished output feels too costly.

## Structure
1. The real problem: raw ideas stay stuck not because of lack of skill but because of friction — the cognitive cost of going from mess to structure
2. The solution: encode your personal presentation standards into a custom prompt once, then wire it to an iPhone shortcut that reads directly from your Notes app
3. How it works in practice: share a note → shortcut runs your prompt → receive a structured slide layout in seconds
4. The deeper principle: this pattern applies to any output type you produce repeatedly — the shortcut doesn't do the thinking, you already did the thinking when you wrote the prompt
