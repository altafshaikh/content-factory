# Post Plan — first-autonoumsly-dveloped-todo-app

## Core Insight
The moment an autonomous agent team takes a PRD and ships a fully working app — plan, code, test, deploy, all without you — is the "Hello World" moment of the AI engineering era.

## Target Audience
Entry-to-mid career software engineers in India who are curious about AI agents and autonomous development workflows — people who still remember the emotional hit of their first working program.

## Structure
1. Hook anchored in the universal "Hello World" memory every developer carries
2. The parallel moment: handing a PRD to an agent team and watching it disappear into four ticks of autonomous work
3. What the agents actually did — plan, execute, test, mark complete — with no human in the loop
4. The output: a working todo app, no interventions, exactly as instructed
5. The shift this signals: we're entering the era where "deploy your first agent team" is the new "Hello World"
