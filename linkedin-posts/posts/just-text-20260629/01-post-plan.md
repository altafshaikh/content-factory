## Core Insight
AI is a multiplier — it makes developers with strong fundamentals dramatically faster, and developers with weak fundamentals dramatically more confused. Your foundations decide which outcome you get.

## Target Audience
Entry-to-mid career software engineers in India (Mumbai, Bengaluru, Delhi) who are overwhelmed by the pace of AI tool releases, actively collecting courses and frameworks, and unsure whether to double down on fundamentals or race to learn the latest tools.

## Structure
1. The shift: a few years ago, learning to code was enough — today it's just table stakes
2. The trap: most developers are optimizing the wrong thing — accumulating tools, not building judgment
3. The multiplier: AI amplifies what you already have (strengths AND weaknesses)
4. The reframe: the skill that compounds for decades is clear thinking and problem-solving — not any specific tool

## CTA
Are you using AI at your work everyday? How has your working style changed in the last 1-2 years?

## Tone Range
Authoritative + conversational — direct without lecturing, grounded in real observation not vague advice
