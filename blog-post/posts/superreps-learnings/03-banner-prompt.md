## Banner Image Prompt

**Core metaphor:** Isolated, glowing fragments floating in dark space — each piece complete on its own, but separated by gaps, no bridges connecting them, suggesting wholeness is still missing.

**Prompt (universal base):**
Dark editorial composition. Deep charcoal background, near black. Several distinct geometric fragments — squares, rectangles, abstract tiles — each lit from within with a soft cool-white or pale cream glow. Scattered across the frame with deliberate negative space between them. No connections, no lines linking the pieces. The fragments feel finished individually but the whole is incomplete. Minimal, atmospheric, modern tech editorial. Wide 16:9 banner format. No text, no logos, no people, no code, no screens.

**Tool modifiers:**
- Midjourney: append `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6`
- DALL·E / GPT-4o: add "digital illustration, flat design, no gradients"
- Ideogram: add "flat vector illustration, editorial style, tech blog"

**Do NOT include:** code on screens, company logos, text or titles, photorealism
