## Banner Image Prompt

**Core metaphor:** One lone figure trying to juggle four roles at once, versus three distinct figures each holding one object and passing it down a line — solo overload vs. a calm relay.

**Prompt (universal base):**
Dark editorial illustration, wide 16:9 format, deep charcoal background. On the left, a single abstract silhouette surrounded by four glowing geometric shapes orbiting it chaotically, overlapping and colliding. On the right, three smaller abstract silhouettes arranged in a horizontal line, each holding exactly one glowing geometric shape, passing it hand to hand down the line in a clean, orderly relay, connected by thin glowing threads. The contrast between chaotic overload on the left and calm, distributed handoff on the right is the entire image. Geometric, minimal, atmospheric. No text, no code, no people's faces, no UI elements, no logos.

**Tool modifiers:**
- Midjourney: append `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6`
- DALL·E / GPT-4o: add "digital illustration, flat design, no gradients"
- Ideogram: add "flat vector illustration, editorial style, tech blog"

**Do NOT include:** code on screens, company logos, text or titles, photorealism
