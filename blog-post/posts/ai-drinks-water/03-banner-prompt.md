## Banner Image Prompt

**Core metaphor:** A pristine glass of clean water sitting next to a glowing server rack, connected by a thin visible channel of water flowing from the rack toward a drain — the hidden cost draining away just out of frame from where anyone would look.

**Prompt (universal base):**
Dark editorial illustration, wide 16:9 format, deep charcoal background. A single glowing server rack stands on the right, faint blue-white light emanating from within. From its base, a thin translucent stream of water flows left across the frame and disappears into a drain at the bottom-left corner. Above the drain, a single water droplet hangs mid-air, catching the server's glow. Negative space dominates the composition — the connection between the machine and the draining water is the entire story. Clean geometric linework, minimal detail, atmospheric lighting. No text, no code, no people, no UI elements, no visible logos.

**Tool modifiers:**
- Midjourney: append `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6`
- DALL·E / GPT-4o: add "digital illustration, flat design, no gradients"
- Ideogram: add "flat vector illustration, editorial style, tech blog"

**Do NOT include:** code on screens, company logos, text or titles, photorealism
