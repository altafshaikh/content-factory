## Banner Image Prompt

**Core metaphor:** A single amplifier or megaphone shape splitting one input into two vastly different sized output waves — one large, controlled, and clean; the other large but distorted and chaotic — representing the same tool amplifying strength into speed and weakness into confusion.

**Prompt (universal base):**
A minimal, dark editorial illustration of one geometric amplifier/funnel shape with a single thin line entering it, splitting into two diverging output paths on the other side — one path resolves into a smooth, clean, confident upward curve, the other dissolves into a tangled, chaotic scribble of lines. Muted dark background, subtle limited color palette, abstract and conceptual, no literal technology imagery, wide banner format.

**Tool modifiers:**
- Midjourney: append `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6`
- DALL·E / GPT-4o: add "digital illustration, flat design, no gradients"
- Ideogram: add "flat vector illustration, editorial style, tech blog"

**Do NOT include:** code on screens, company logos, text or titles, photorealism
