# Reel Plan — hello-world-ai-agents

## Style & Format
Style: FACELESS — The content is an autonomous pipeline completing tasks. Screen-recording shows this in real-time with no face needed; on-screen text carries the emotional beats. On-camera would add friction without adding trust here — the proof (the app running) is the trust signal.
Format: screen-demo
Length: 30s — four ticks need individual screen time to feel demonstrated rather than asserted, plus a payoff reveal. 15s is too tight for a demo with a proper reframe; 30s lands each beat without overstaying.
Audio: trending — a "satisfying build" or "level-up completion" style track. Silent-viewer text overlays carry all meaning; trending audio rides the reach algorithm. Search vibe: "satisfying completion / tech montage build / rising synth with clean clicks."

## Core Insight
A team of autonomous agents can replace every step of solo development — plan, execute, test, ship — if given a precise spec. The developer's job is upstream now.

## Target Viewer
A software engineer (junior to senior) who has heard the agent hype but hasn't seen one close a complete task end-to-end, start to finish. They're skeptical. The app running is the proof.

## Hook (0-2s)
**On-screen text:** "Solo developers are a bottleneck. Agent teams are the fix."
**Spoken/VO:** "Solo developers are the bottleneck. Agent teams are the fix — and they already work."
**Visual:** Split-frame or fast cut: a solo developer staring at a screen → a pipeline dashboard with three agent labels lighting up in sequence. Motion from frame 1.

## Beat Skeleton
1. **Hook (0-2s)** — contrarian claim + visual of solo-dev vs agent-team split. Scroll-stopper.
2. **The pivot (2-8s)** — "You don't need a larger team. You need a smarter one." Agent-team diagram: one spec in → three agents out.
3. **Tick 1-2 (8-12s)** — screen-recording: planner agent outputting tasks / executor writing code. On-screen: "Tick 1 — Planning" / "Tick 2 — Building." Fast cuts.
4. **Tick 3-4 (12-16s)** — tester running / "All tasks complete." On-screen: "Tick 3 — Testing" / "Tick 4 — Done." Hold the terminal line briefly.
5. **Reframe (16-22s)** — "This is the Hello World of agent teams." Browser opens: working to-do app. Brief click-through.
6. **Implication (22-28s)** — "If they can close a full task now — what's next?" Hold on the app.
7. **CTA (28-30s)** — "Comment: what would YOU give them to build?" Comment-bait.

## Caption direction
First-line hook: "Solo developers are a bottleneck. Agent teams aren't science fiction anymore."
Body: 3-agent team (planner, executor, tester), one product spec, 4 ticks, zero intervention, working app.
CTA: "What would you give your team to build?"
