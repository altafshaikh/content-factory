### Caption

The moment AI agent development became real for me happened when I walked away.

I gave a 3-agent team (planner, executor, tester) a product spec for a to-do app. They planned the work, wrote the code, tested everything they built, and shipped it — 4 ticks, no intervention from me in between.

When I came back, it was running. That feeling — the same one as running your first Hello World — came back.

This is the Hello World moment for AI agent teams: the first time a spec goes in and a working product comes out, completely autonomously.

Following along? I post one of these weekly.
