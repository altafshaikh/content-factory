## Caption

Solo developers are a bottleneck. Agent teams aren't science fiction anymore.

I gave a 3-agent team (planner, executor, tester) one product spec. They built a working to-do app in 4 ticks — planned it, wrote the code, tested it, and marked it done.

Zero intervention from me between any of them.

This is the Hello World moment for AI agent teams. Same feeling as running your first program — except the agents did the running.

What would you give your team to build?
