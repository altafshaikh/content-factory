### Caption

For months, I had ideas trapped in my Notes app.

Every time I tried to turn one into a presentation, the friction stopped me. Messy text. Blank deck. Too many steps between them.

So I built a custom iPhone shortcut: share any note, AI applies my pre-written slide structure, clean outline comes out. Setup took 30 minutes. It removed a bottleneck I'd had for over a year.

The ideas were never the problem. The activation energy of formatting them was.

Design around friction. Not through it.

Save this to build yours. What's the idea you've had the longest that still hasn't become anything?
