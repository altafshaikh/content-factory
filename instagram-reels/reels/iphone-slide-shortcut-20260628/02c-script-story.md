### Caption

For months, I had ideas trapped in my Notes app.

Not because I didn't care about them — because every time I sat down to turn one into slides, the messy text, the blank deck, the number of steps... I'd close the laptop and tell myself "later."

I built a custom iPhone shortcut to fix it: share any note, AI applies my own slide structure rules, clean outline comes out. Setup took 30 minutes. It removed a blocker I'd had for over a year.

The ideas weren't the problem. The friction was.

Save this to build your own. What's the idea you've had the longest that still hasn't become anything?
